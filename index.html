About

aadarshbanjade.computer@gmail.com github.com/blissed-out

I'm a self-taught software developer who spent years building full-stack systems and web applications. Recently I made a deliberate choice to step away from professional software development and redirect my energy toward something I find deeply compelling — AI research.

Right now I'm at the very beginning: rebuilding mathematical foundations from scratch. Calculus, linear algebra, probability. The kind of math I skipped past to get to code quickly. Turns out, you eventually have to come back for it.

The goal is to eventually read, understand, and contribute to machine learning research. I don't know exactly what area yet — I'm early enough that I'm still figuring out what questions interest me most.

I write code in Neovim. I think slowly and take notes. I'm in no rush.
